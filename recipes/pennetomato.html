Penne with Tomatosauce

Description

Easy and Tasty

Ingridients

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • dry chilli
  • Whipped Cream
  • Tomatoconcentrate
  • Red Wine
  • Penne

Steps

  1. Boil Water and put Penne into it
  2. Cut Onions and Garlic and chilli (if you like spicy
  3. Heat olive oil in pan
  4. put Onions into pan
  5. put Garlic into pan
  6. drip little bit of red wine into pan and some Tomatoconcentrate
  7. when the onions and garlic are red and begin a little bit to burn add slowly some whipped cream and mix it with the ingredients till its creamy
  8. repeat this process as often as you like
  9. before taking the penne out of the boiling water drip some boiling water into the sauce for extra taste
