Lasagna

Description

The best Lasagna is made by Mama.

Ingridients

  • Bolognese (Recipe coming soon)
  • Lasagna Plates
  • Bechamele Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make Bolognese
  2. Make Bechamele Sauce
  3. Get an stove bowl and fill a tiny layer of Bolognese in it
  4. on top of the layer drip some bechamele sauce
  5. place on top of that Lasagna Plates
  6. repeat this till nearly the whole bowl is full
  7. the last layer will be only Bolognese and Bechamele sauce, instead of Lasagna plates you put cheese on top of the last layer.
