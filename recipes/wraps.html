Wraps

Description

Fast and Delicous

Ingridients

  • Wraps
  • Sauces
  • Shrooms
  • Vegan Schnitzel
  • Paprika
  • Cucumber
  • Salad
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the Vegan Schnitzel in the Pan and cut them to little pieces (big like the middle finger)
  2. Heat the Wraps in the stove or microwave with cheese on them
  3. cut vegetables into little pieces like schnitzels
  4. take out the wraps and put sauce on it
  5. place the schnitzels and vegetables on it
  6. roll it all together and have a great peace of meal
